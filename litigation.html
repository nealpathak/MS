Our Approach to Litigation

Mills Shirley LLP’s litigation team is committed to defending our clients’ interests in a wide array of disputes. With a deep understanding of Texas courts and decades of combined experience, we navigate complex litigation efficiently, aiming for favorable resolutions that align with our clients’ strategic goals.

Areas of Focus

  • Commercial Litigation: Breach of contract, business torts, partnership disputes
  • Construction Disputes: Design defects, construction defects, contractor/subcontractor issues
  • Maritime & Admiralty Disputes: Claims involving maritime contracts, cargo issues, maritime liens
  • Real Estate & Land Use Litigation
  • Insurance Defense & Coverage Disputes

Whether through negotiation, mediation, arbitration, or trial, we tailor our strategies to the specific needs of each case, striving to achieve results that preserve our clients’ resources and reputations.
